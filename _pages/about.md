My name is Siyuan Liang, you can also call me Kyrie.

I am currently a postdoctoral research fellow supervised by <a href="https://tsungyiho.github.io">Prof. Tsung-Yi Ho</a>. My study interests include Design Automation, Optimization, Machine Learning, Microfluidics, and Advanced Packaging. You can find more details about me in the corresponding News, Publications, Teaching, and CV pages.

I joined Xi'an Jiaotong University in 2018, majoring in Electrical Engineering and Automation (Elite Class), and was awarded the Yuejie Academic Elite Scholarship of 240,000 CNY. I got my bacholar's degree and became an honorary graduate of Qian Xuesen Honors College in 2022. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Engineering from the Chinese University of Hong Kong with a dissertation entitled "Beyond Trial and Error: Reliable and Efficient Microfluidicis via Systematic Design".

Please feel free to contact me via email, I will do my best to reply promptly.

It's really nice to meet you, wish you a lovely day!
